2. What Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Work logs — shift dates, platform (Uber, Bolt, etc.), hours and earnings you enter manually
  • Mileage logs — trip dates, distances and descriptions you record
  • Expenses — amounts, categories and descriptions you enter
  • Settings — your in-app preferences
  • Receipt images — photos you choose to upload (stored encrypted)
  • Device identifier — a randomly generated ID stored locally on your device to enable cloud sync

3. Bank Account Sync (Plaid) — Optional

Bank account synchronisation is off by default and requires your explicit consent to activate. You can disconnect at any time.

If you choose to enable bank sync, we use Plaid (an FCA-authorised open banking provider) to access a read-only view of your bank transactions. Specifically, we access:

  • Transaction date, amount, and merchant name
  • Transaction category (to identify earnings and deductible expenses)

We do not access, store, or transmit:

  • Your bank account number or sort code
  • Your bank login credentials
  • Your account balance
  • Any transactions unrelated to your gig work activity

Disconnecting bank sync immediately revokes Plaid's access token and permanently deletes all imported transaction data from our systems.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To calculate your earnings, expenses, mileage, and estimated tax liability
  • To sync your data securely across your devices
  • To provide insights and summaries within the app

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose other than providing Driver Buddy's core functionality.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR)

  • Contract — processing your logs and sync data to deliver the service you signed up for
  • Consent — bank account synchronisation via Plaid (explicit opt-in, withdrawable at any time)
  • Legitimate interests — app performance monitoring and error reporting

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Cloudflare's infrastructure (D1 database and R2 object storage), which provides encryption at rest and in transit. All communication between your device and our servers uses TLS 1.2 or higher. Access to production systems is protected by multi-factor authentication.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is immediate and irreversible — all records are purged from our database and storage.

If you disconnect bank sync, all Plaid-imported transaction data is deleted immediately, independent of your account.

8. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent — for bank sync, at any time, without affecting other services
  • Object — to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any right, contact us at cvc.chaudhary@purelivingpro.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Plaid — open banking provider (FCA authorised). Used only when bank sync is enabled. Plaid's Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — infrastructure, storage, and CDN provider
  • Sentry — anonymised error reporting for app stability

10. Children's Privacy

Driver Buddy is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.
